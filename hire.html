Come to the Reindeer Farm for photo sessions

The Reindeer Farm in Shortsville, NY is a great venue for fun and unique engagement photos, family photos, holiday cards and more. We provide the reindeer, sleighs, and a variety of festive and rustic backgrounds around the farm.

To book your own private photo session, where you bring your own photographer, text Mike at (585) 282-9892. Private photo sessions are $200 per hour, one hour minimum.
