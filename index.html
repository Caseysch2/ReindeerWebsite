Welcome to the Shortsville Reindeer Farm. We are a tiny little farm in a tiny little town in Western New York. We started a small a herd of reindeer in 2017. Why reindeer? Well... who wouldn't want a pet reindeer? These gentle, social animals enjoy following us around as we work in the pasture and add a sense of wonder to our days. Today we share the joy by bringing our pets out to community events and hosting events on the farm. Visits are by appointment only.
