Welcome to the Shortsville Reindeer Farm. We are a tiny little farm in a tiny little town in Western New York. We started a small a herd of reindeer in 2017. Why reindeer? Well... who wouldn't want a pet reindeer? These gentle, social animals enjoy following us around as we work in the pasture and add a sense of wonder to our days. Today we share the joy by bringing our pets out to community events and hosting events on the farm.

Visit the farm. Join us for events and classes year round including holiday visits with the reindeer; classes in beekeeping, maple syrup and cider making... and more. Reservations required. Check the events page
