About the Shortsville Reindeer Farm

In 2017 Mike Schaertl, feeling as if he had not yet fully realized his dream of being a world-renowned eccentric, decided that what his life was really missing was some mystical ungulates. With the unparalled patience and help of his wife the octagonal barn went up and the first reindeer arrived. Today there are three reindeer living at the Shortsville Reindeer Farm: Little Brother, Little Buddy, and Moose.
